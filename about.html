Our Story

Where Morocco Meets Qatar

A union of two rich cultures, brought to life through the purest Moroccan ingredients

Who We Are

More Than Just A Brand

Arganity is more than a skincare brand — it is a symbol of cultural union between Qatar and Morocco. Born from a deep love for authentic Moroccan beauty traditions, we bring the most precious ingredients of the Atlas Mountains directly to Doha.

We work directly with Moroccan cooperatives to source the finest argan oil, prickly pear seed oil, cannabis oil, and natural clay — all 100% organic, cold-pressed, and ethically harvested. Every product is a bridge between two worlds, crafted to honour both.

Our formulations are rooted in centuries-old Moroccan beauty rituals, reimagined for the modern woman. We believe that luxury and nature are not opposites — they are one and the same.

Our Values
The principles that guide everything we create
🌿
100% Organic

Every ingredient we use is certified organic. No synthetic additives, no harmful chemicals — just pure, potent botanicals that work in harmony with your skin.

From Atlas Mountains

Our ingredients are sourced directly from the Atlas Mountains of Morocco — one of the world's most biodiverse regions. Cold-pressed and minimally processed to preserve every nutrient.

Qatar–Morocco Union

Arganity is a celebration of the cultural and spiritual bond between Qatar and Morocco. We honour both nations through our products, our people, and our story.

Our Mission

Pure Beauty, Rooted in Tradition

Our mission is to offer the women of Qatar and the Gulf region access to the world's most powerful organic skincare — oils and formulations that have nourished Moroccan women for generations.

We are committed to transparency, sustainability, and the empowerment of the Moroccan communities who harvest these precious gifts of nature. When you choose Arganity, you choose integrity from seed to skin.
