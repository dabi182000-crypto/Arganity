Our Story

Rooted in Nature, Crafted with Purpose

From Founder
"

Arganity is more than just a brand, it represents a symbol of the harmonious rapprochement of cultures and civilizations between the beloved State of Qatar and the honorable Kingdom of Morocco. Mesmerized by the wealth and beauty of the native people of southwestern Morocco: Our journey began! Their liquid gold "Argan" and organic Mediterranean spices were so exquisite that the idea of sharing them with the world became a personal mission. Thus, Arganity was born on December 2018 — a brand that celebrates the ancient wisdom and natural beauty of our region.

Thanks to the grace of God in a short period of time, Arganity has become an important player in the Qatari market for natural, organic skin and hair care products. This is a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainability.

At Arganity, we hold ourselves to the highest standard of excellence when it comes to fulfilling the aspirations of our esteemed customers, we remain steadfast in our unrelenting pursuit of our mission, we share the captivating beauty and rich cultural legacy of Morocco with the rest of the world.

Our Story
FROM THE ATLAS MOUNTAINS TO YOU

Arganity is a luxury organic skin and hair care brand inspired by the richness of nature and the ancestral beauty secrets of Morocco. Rooted in the Atlas Mountains, our philosophy blends traditional rituals with modern innovation to deliver powerful, natural results.

We carefully source therapeutic ingredients grown organically in the Atlas Mountain orchards, where wild seeds and herbs are hand-selected and sun-dried to preserve their full potency. Our oils are 100% organic, cold-pressed, and unrefined, ensuring that every nutrient remains intact and effective.

At Arganity, we create expert-level skincare that nurtures both body and soul. Our formulas are designed to promote healthy, radiant skin while offering a calming, sensorial experience. Each product reflects our commitment to purity, performance, and respect for nature.

Through close collaboration with certified laboratories and skilled artisans, we combine innovative extraction techniques with time-honored methods. Our products are clean, dermatologically tested, and thoughtfully formulated to minimize environmental impact.

Arganity embraces a holistic approach to beauty — focusing on root-cause care and integrated wellness rituals. From prickly pear and argan oils to aromatherapy and traditional Moroccan treatments, every creation is an invitation to reconnect with nature and experience beauty in its purest form.
